definitionThe status of a sample or specimen in relation to curation processes (e.g. the recording and organization of items in a collection), both before and after storage/archiving, and with respect to its display or storage location at a specific point in time.
history noteCompiled by the Geological Survey of Western Australia to assist in the management of its physical sample/specimen collections. GSWA distinguishes samples from specimens on the basis of the latter including both primary and derived materials and having been accessioned into a suitable management system.
