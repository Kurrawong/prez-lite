definitionA description of the intended configuration and trajectory of a borehole, covering concepts from the energy and mineral resources industry. Note that most of the concepts in this vocabulary apply to both the process of drilling (e.g. directional drilling), as well as to an actual borehole (e.g. directional well), even if the definitions in this vocabulary focus specifically on the latter.
