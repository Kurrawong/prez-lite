Metadata

definitionThis vocabulary lists the fundamental types of geological features that are used to define the geological framework/4D evolution of Western Australia and are relevant to the duties of the Geological Survey of Western Australia. A geological feature is a physical or conceptual feature that is hypothesized to exist coherently in the Earth and that results from geological processes (definition largely derived from SWEET ontology http://sweetontology.net/realmGeol#geologicfeature).
